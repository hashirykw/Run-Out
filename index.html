Who We Are

Karachi's Rooftop Cricket Hub

Run Out is North Nazimabad's go-to rooftop indoor sports arena — a venue built for the way Karachiites love cricket. We took the energy of street cricket and brought it indoors, elevated it to a rooftop, and made it available around the clock, every single day of the year.

Whether it's a late-night match with the squad, a weekend tournament with your corporate team, or serious batting practice, Run Out delivers the pitch, the gear, and the atmosphere. Our staff are sports fans first — always on hand to help you set up and get playing.
