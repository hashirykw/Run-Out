Who We Are

Karachi's Finest Rooftop Arena

Run Out is North Nazimabad's go-to rooftop indoor sports arena — built for the way Karachiites love cricket. We took the raw energy of street cricket, elevated it to a rooftop, and made it available every hour of every day.

Whether it's a late-night match with your squad, a weekend corporate tournament, or focused batting practice — Run Out delivers the pitch, the atmosphere, and the service. Our team are sports lovers first, always ready to set up and get you on the field.
